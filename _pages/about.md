I received my master's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Hunan University, where I worked with Prof. [Xiangxiang Zeng](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=B20HBMIAAAAJ&hl=en) and Prof. [Yuansheng Liu](https://scholar.google.co.uk/citations?user=H6yrNMQAAAAJ&hl=en). Prior to that, I completed my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology at China University of Mining and Technology.

My research focused on graph machine learning for drug discovery and explainable AI. I am also currently working on trustworthy large language models for biomedical applications.
